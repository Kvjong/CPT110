Project idea:



Overview:


This project idea is a proposal for a PC game that I have always wanted to play for over a decade. It is a game that encompasses multiple genre in RPG and strategy that makes this idea truly remarkable. In terms of strategy, it is a conquering type of game, but with a first person role and with the option of virtual reality to make it truly immersive. This first game pitch will be of certain size serving as a prototype, and scalable depending on the funds received that will allow the scope to expand. So as a first person, you will start in a location and eventually conquer territories by building war machines and enlisting soldiers. All this will be done in a first person perspective.



Motivation:


The scope of this game would be satisfying for players that enjoy strategy and role playing role into a game that is truly immersive. Most games are sequel to their previous generations and just revamped to appear more appealing, but essentially they are more of the same, such as Battlefield 1, 2, 3 4 and so forth. Game studios prefer to make games that have seen success and understand that their games have been tried and tested thus the status quo. Webb (2019) contends that the games industry is in excess of $120 billion industry and growing rapidly and Microsoft estimating around 2 billion gamers and by 2022, the forecast could place the industry at around the $200 billion mark.



Description:


The Concept of the game is a strategy game of conquering area / land / zone and hence, increasing an empire perse. It is done done from a first person perspective, where you are the character that will form an army, construct civic buildings, invade, defend and move around the world in your territory. You will have ministers, advisers and generals that will help with your quest, and you can become the King, Queen, President, Fuhrer, Dictator or become a General and work for your Country. The game setting is flexible in terms of time periods, such as Medieval, Gun powder, Modern, Futuristic or even fantasy world is applicable. Paired with a Virtual Reality system such as an Oculus or the HTC, and the game will be immersive by Stankovik (2016, pp.67-85) and further argues that the User interface typically used for a monitor setup will not necessarily transition to the virtual reality setup. The typical Role playing games like the Elder Scrolls, Mount & Blade or 3d shooting games such as Battlefield 5 will be more suitable for this typical games pitch.


Initially, the game will be a sandbox meaning the pc game will be installed on a local computer and played locally without the need for an internet connection. The player will start at a particular zone and from a chosen story line will resume that role. From there, they will gather and build the necessary resources to expand, such as building contacts by hiring ministers, advisers to take care of certain aspects of the game. They can conduct research and further improve or unlock new technologies to further expand their empire. The concept of this game can expand to the scale of planetary size through the concept of an expansion pack. The scope of this game can expand even further such as an intergalactic empire spanning many worlds utilising procedural generational. However, Rusch (2016, pp.32-51) argues that tracing the human experience is an art and finding that theme that has a balance between feature rich content or the interface is cluttered with buttons, will alter the game experience, so the scope and sheer potential for this game is proportional to user experience.



However, the pitch for this game should have a level of immersiveness and for players to realise the potential for this type of game. Madigan (2016, pp.127-144) further asserts immersive has a correlation with spatial awareness that can transport the player into the game and become part of the game experience. This means as players expand and delve deeper into the game, the map can be broken down into zones for player to expand into. For example in a piece of land, there may be five zones and across the ocean, there are further zones in that landmass. Players will expand into those zones and through diplomacy feature, they may have an alliance, cultural exchange or annexation of that zone.
